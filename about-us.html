About Us

The ever beautiful venue, the Universe banquet hall is a beautiful place to hold your memorable event. The premium facilities offered by the venue are double the cherry on the cake. The parking space, lavish interiors, in house decor and catering services are all to watch out for in this mind blowing banquet. The entrance of the Universe banquet Andheri is one to watch out for with lush green trees outside the banquet and a decent parking space of 10 cars. The banquet has a great ambience with ample pre function area, wonderful chandeliers and classy warm lights. The Italian marble adds more class to the venue. The Universe Banquet Andheri offers you its in house experienced team to cater to your needs accurately and provide you delicious multi cuisines. The specialty of the Universe Banquet hall is its innovative starters, in house decor facilities and an ample space for parking. The venue is widely known for holding Gujarati, North Indian and Maharashtrian weddings. The overnight wedding facility and in house catering service are a good attraction for the venue.
